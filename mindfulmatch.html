Mindful Match: Wellness Dating App

As the lead of a five-person team for my Business Communications Capstone project, I developed Mindful Match, a proposed sister app to Tinder. Our app focuses on fostering meaningful connections through shared health and wellness goals. Our team conducted in-depth market and user research to identify a gap in the dating app market to develop a solution that prioritizes genuine connections over superficial matches.
