Rice CRISPR Treat

For this project, I developed a computational framework to improve detection of off-target CRISPR-Cas9 edits using deep sequencing data and homology-targeted integration. I compiled genome sequencing datasets, ran next-generation sequencing analysis to flag abnormalities, and trained a model to compare those abnormalities against known off-target sites.
